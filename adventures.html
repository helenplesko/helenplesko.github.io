HELEN PLESKO

ONTARIO PROVINCIAL PARKS

My love for the outdoors started at the young age of four, when my parents took me on a week-long camping trip at Killbear Provincial Park. My favourite trail to bike along was the Lighthouse Point Trail from the Kilcoursie Campgrounds. We often saw deer munching on the foliage on the way to the lighthouse, where we sat and listened to the waves crash the rocky shore.
